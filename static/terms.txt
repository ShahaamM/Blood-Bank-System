Terms and Conditions

1. Eligibility
1.1. You must be at least 18 years old to donate blood.
1.2. You must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
1.3. You must be in good health and feeling well on the day of donation.
1.4. You must not have donated blood within the last 56 days.

2. Health and Medical Information
2.1. You agree to provide accurate and complete information regarding your health and medical history.
2.2. You consent to undergo a brief health screening and hemoglobin test before donation.
2.3. You acknowledge that certain medical conditions or medications may temporarily or permanently defer you from donating blood.

3. Donation Process
3.1. You understand that the blood donation process includes registration, a health screening, the blood draw, and a short recovery period.
3.2. You agree to follow all instructions provided by the medical staff during the donation process.
3.3. You acknowledge that you may experience mild side effects such as dizziness, bruising, or light-headedness after donation.

4. Confidentiality
4.1. Your personal and medical information will be kept confidential and used solely for the purposes of determining your eligibility to donate blood.
4.2. Your information will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

5. Use of Donated Blood
5.1. You understand that your donated blood will be tested for infectious diseases, including HIV and hepatitis.
5.2. You consent to your blood being used for transfusion to patients in need, medical research, or other medical purposes as deemed necessary by the blood bank.

6. Right to Refuse
6.1. The blood donation center reserves the right to defer or refuse donors based on eligibility criteria and medical guidelines.
6.2. You understand that your donation may be declined if it does not meet the required standards.

7. Liability
7.1. You acknowledge that you are donating blood voluntarily and at your own risk.
7.2. The blood donation center and its staff are not liable for any adverse reactions or complications arising from your donation, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

8. Consent
8.1. By signing this registration form, you consent to donate blood and agree to abide by the terms and conditions stated above.
8.2. You confirm that you have read and understood these terms and conditions and that all information provided is truthful and accurate.
